Game Rules

  • Rock beats scissors, scissors beat paper, and paper beats rock.
  • Decide whether you’ll count off “rock, paper, scissors, shoot” or just “rock, paper, scissors” before playing.
  • Use rock, paper, scissors to make decisions or simply for fun.
  • If both players choose the same hand, each player lays down another hand.
